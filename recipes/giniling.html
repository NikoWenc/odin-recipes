Pork Giniling

Description

Pork Giniling is a Filipino ground pork stew cooked with tomatoes, potatoes, carrots, and green peas. It's a savory, mildly sweet dish often served with rice, perfect for everyday meals.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground pork
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 medium tomatoes, chopped (or ½ cup canned diced tomatoes)
  • 1 medium potato, diced
  • 1 small carrot, diced
  • 1/3 cup green peas (fresh or canned)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste or tomato sauce
  • 1–2 tsp fish sauce (optional, adds umami)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cooking oil
    • Water or broth, as needed
  • Water or broth, as needed

Steps for cooking

  1. Sauté Aromatics
    • Heat oil in a pan. Sauté garlic until fragrant, then add onion and cook until soft.
    • Add tomatoes and cook until they break down (or use canned).
  2. Brown the Ground Pork
    • Add ground pork. Cook until no longer pink and slightly browned.
    • Season with soy sauce, fish sauce (if using), salt, and pepper.
  3. Add Tomato Paste/Sauce
    • Stir in tomato paste or sauce. Mix well to coat the meat.
  4. Simmer with Vegetables
    • Add diced potatoes and carrots. Pour in a bit of water or broth to help cook them.
    • Simmer for 10–15 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
  5. Add Green Peas
    • Toss in green peas during the last few minutes of cooking.
    • Adjust seasoning if needed.
